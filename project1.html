Project 1

The objective of the game is to be the first player to get to 100 points. Player 1 always starts off the game. The player whose turn it is throws a die and scores as many points as the total shown on the die providing the die doesnâ€™t roll a one. The player may continue rolling and accumulating points (but risk rolling a one) or end his turn. When the player decides to end their turn, their total score gets incremented by their current score. If the player rolls a 1 his turn is over, he loses all points he accumulated that turn, and he passes the die to the next player. Play passes from player to player until a winner is determined.

This application was built using HTML, CSS, and Javascript with DOM manipulation and used the Agile methodology
